About MAK

Mak Labels & Packaging Solution is a leading manufacture of self Adhesive Labels, Barcode Tags, Boarding Tickets, Preprinted Labels,Speciality Labels & Security Labels established in 2014

We are the wholesaler & retailer of VCI Films, LDPE Film, LDPE Sheet, Stretch Film, Paper Tube, Paper Core, EPE Foam Roll, EPE FoamSheet, EPE Foam Pouch and Air Bubble Sheet. 

With humble beginnings in 2015, MAK is now a specialty flexible packaging company having built its reputation on timely deliveries, unwavering customer service, effective partnership, unmatched quality and customer focused innovation.     We offer cost effective and value added specialty packaging solutions to all our customers. The secret of our success in the individual attention that we give to each of our customers for understanding their needs and meeting their expectations by delivering consistent value through focused innovation. Our dedicated workforce has made ASP one of the most preferred specialty flexible packaging partners for the industry.  

Today, we are one of the leading flexible packaging companies in South Region primarily catering to the local market

 We believe in being close to all our customers and prospects. To meet the customer requirement in an efficient and effective way, MAK has set up manufacturing facilities in Chennai (Tamil Nadu). We have also set up application and business development team’s in Chennai, South Tamilnadu.

The company ensures that the packaging is strong enough to protect the product from damage or contamination by other microorganisms.
